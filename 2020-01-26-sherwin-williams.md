<h2 style="text-align: center;">Sherwin Williams Paint</h2>
<h3 style="text-align: center;">Klahanie Discount Code</h3>
<h2 style="text-align: center;">#5462</h2>
<h3>Sherwin Williams is including all interior or exterior paints or stains. The discounts range from 15% on brushes and equipment, up to 46% off the Duration line of paints. Presently this is only offered at the Pine Lake retail store, but soon will be expanded to other area SW stores.</h3>
<h3>1. The paint/stain must be purchased at the Sherwin Williams store located at 3000 Issaquah Pine Lake road SE. You can also order on the phone by calling 425.313.0490</h3>
<h3>2. The discount is for Klahanie residents who mention our wholesale discount program- you may be asked to show your ID with a Klahanie address.</h3>
<h3>3. You pay for the paint and supplies you order just like any regular purchase.</h3>
<h3>4. If you have any questions, ask to speak with Akeem Bell, the store manager.</h3>
